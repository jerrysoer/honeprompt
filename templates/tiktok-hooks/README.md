# TikTok Hooks

Write scroll-stopping TikTok video hooks that feel spoken, not scripted.

**Category:** creators
**Author:** HonePrompt Team
**Estimated Cost:** $1-3 (25 iterations)
**Tags:** tiktok, social-media, hooks, short-form-video, creators

## What This Template Does

Optimizes a TikTok hook-writing prompt. The prompt takes a video topic as input and generates a punchy opening line (2-3 seconds of spoken content) designed to prevent swiping. Unlike written hooks, TikTok hooks must sound natural when spoken aloud and match the energy of the content niche.

## Scoring Criteria

- **Hook strength (35%)** — Does it create an immediate pattern interrupt?
- **Brevity (25%)** — Can it be spoken in 2-3 seconds? Under 15 words?
- **Conversational tone (25%)** — Does it sound like someone talking, not writing?
- **Platform awareness (15%)** — Does it match TikTok energy and niche conventions?
