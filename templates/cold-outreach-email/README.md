# Cold Outreach Email

Write personalized, under-100-word cold emails that earn replies.

**Category:** saas
**Author:** HonePrompt Team
**Estimated Cost:** $1-3 (25 iterations)
**Tags:** sales, outreach, email, cold-email, personalization

## What This Template Does

Optimizes a system prompt that writes cold outreach emails for SaaS sales, recruiting, partnerships, and professional networking. The optimizer pushes toward genuine personalization, ruthless brevity, and low-commitment CTAs that make replying easy.

## Scoring Criteria

- **Personalization (30%)** — Specific, non-transferable references to the prospect
- **Brevity (25%)** — Under 100 words with zero filler phrases
- **CTA Clarity (20%)** — One low-commitment ask, clearly stated
- **Human Tone (15%)** — Peer-to-peer, conversational, not salesy or desperate
- **Subject Line (10%)** — Under 6 words, specific, not clickbaity
