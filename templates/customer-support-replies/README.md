# Customer Support Replies

Write empathetic, resolution-focused customer support email replies.

**Category:** saas
**Author:** HonePrompt Team
**Estimated Cost:** $1-3 (25 iterations)
**Tags:** support, customer-success, email, empathy, saas

## What This Template Does

Optimizes a system prompt that writes customer support email replies from complaint or question context. The optimizer pushes toward replies that balance genuine empathy with clear, concrete resolutions -- avoiding both template-speak and over-apologizing.

## Scoring Criteria

- **Resolution Clarity (30%)** — Specific actions, concrete timelines, no vague promises
- **Empathy & Acknowledgment (25%)** — Customer feels heard, not processed
- **Tone & Professionalism (20%)** — Warm but competent, no corporate jargon
- **Completeness (15%)** — Every question addressed, follow-ups anticipated
- **Conciseness (10%)** — 100-200 words, every sentence earns its place
