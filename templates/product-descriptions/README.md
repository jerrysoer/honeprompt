# Product Descriptions

Write benefit-driven product descriptions for e-commerce and SaaS landing pages.

**Category:** marketers
**Author:** HonePrompt Team
**Estimated Cost:** $1-3 (25 iterations)
**Tags:** copywriting, e-commerce, conversion, product-marketing

## What This Template Does

Optimizes a system prompt that writes compelling product descriptions from product details and target audience info. The optimizer pushes the prompt away from feature-listing toward benefit-driven, emotionally resonant copy that follows the AIDA framework.

## Scoring Criteria

- **Benefit Orientation (30%)** — Customer outcomes over technical specs
- **Readability & Scannability (20%)** — Short paragraphs, clear language, 80-150 word range
- **Emotional Resonance (20%)** — Sensory language, mental imagery, pain point connection
- **CTA Effectiveness (15%)** — Specific, low-friction, product-appropriate calls to action
- **Audience Fit (15%)** — Tone and vocabulary matched to the target buyer
